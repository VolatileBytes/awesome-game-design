# Progression Design — Hollow Knight

Progression is multi-axis: HP (masks), Soul capacity (vessels), nail damage (upgrades), traversal abilities (tools), passive modifiers (charms), spells (magic), and lore (essence + dream content). All earned through exploration and combat — no XP system.

## No XP or Levels

Critically, Hollow Knight has NO experience points, NO character levels, NO skill trees. Progression is ENTIRELY gear + geographic exploration.

- Defeat bosses → receive ability.
- Find shards → aggregate upgrades.
- Buy/find charms → equip passives.

This preserves design integrity: enemies don't scale, numbers don't inflate.

## Progression Axes

### Masks (HP)

- Start: 5 masks.
- Mask Shards: 16 total, 4 per +1 mask.
- Max base: 9 masks (via 16/16 shards).
- Plus Fragile Heart / Unbreakable Heart charm: +2 → 11 max.

**Mask Shards locations (examples)**:
- Sly shopkeep sells 4 (progressively).
- Hidden in Forgotten Crossroads.
- Enter Grimm Troupe questline.
- Hidden behind walls in various zones.
- Colosseum trials.

### Soul Vessels

- Start: 3 vessel orbs (99 Soul capacity).
- Vessel Fragments: 9 total, 3 per +1 vessel.
- Max base: 5 vessels (165 Soul).
- Plus charms for Soul capacity upgrades.

**Vessel Fragment locations (examples)**:
- Grubfather rewards.
- Seer (essence-trade).
- Hidden behind walls.
- Colosseum trials.

### Nail Upgrades (Nailsmith)

Linear damage path:

| Nail | Damage | Cost | Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|
| Old Nail | 5 | Start | — |
| Sharpened Nail | 9 | 250 Geo + 1 Pale Ore | Nailsmith |
| Channeled Nail | 13 | 800 Geo + 2 Pale Ore | Nailsmith |
| Coiled Nail | 17 | 2000 Geo + 3 Pale Ore | Nailsmith |
| Pure Nail | 21 | 4000 Geo + 4 Pale Ore | Nailsmith |

Pale Ore (6 in world) is the rare gate:
- Crystal Peak (crystal vein).
- Deep Nest (Nosk area).
- Colosseum of Fools (Warrior trial).
- Hallownest's Crown (stag station reward).
- Seer (essence trade).
- Grubfather (46-grub reward).

### Spells (Learn from Statues)

Three spell families, each with base + upgrade:

**Fireball**:
- Vengeful Spirit → Shade Soul (Soul Sanctum after Soul Master beat → second visit).

**Dive**:
- Desolate Dive (Soul Master reward) → Descending Dark (Crystal Peak lifeblood chamber).

**Wraiths**:
- Howling Wraiths (Howling Cliffs hidden) → Abyss Shriek (Abyss ability).

All require Dream Nail mechanism to upgrade to higher forms in some cases; varies per spell line.

### Charms (Passive Modifiers)

45 charms total. Key categories:

**Combat**:
- Unbreakable Strength (Leg Eater fix) → +50% nail damage.
- Quick Slash: faster attacks.
- Longnail / Mark of Pride: reach+.
- Steady Body: no recoil.
- Fragile Heart / Unbreakable Heart: +2 masks.
- Shaman Stone: spell size + damage.

**Defense**:
- Stalwart Shell: longer i-frames.
- Deep Focus: 2x heal per Focus.
- Baldur Shell: blocks damage while focusing.
- Joni's Blessing: +4 lifeblood, disable Focus.
- Heavy Blow: bigger knockback.

**Exploration**:
- Gathering Swarm: auto-pickup Geo.
- Wayward Compass: player on map.
- Sprintmaster: faster run.
- Dashmaster: dash cooldown + down-dash.

**Synergy**:
- Grubberfly's Elegy: at full HP, nail shoots beam.
- Kingsoul / Void Heart: required for true ending.
- Nailmaster's Glory: Nail-Art charge speed+.
- Quick Focus: faster heal.
- Spell Twister: spells cost -24% soul.

**Companion**:
- Grimmchild (Grimm Troupe): companion that attacks.
- Weaversong: weaverlings.
- Glowing Womb: summons hatchling.

### Charm Notches

Equip slots. Start: 3. Max: 11.
- Sly sells some.
- Salubra sells four (one per 40% charm progression).
- Grimm Troupe gives one.
- Colosseum reward.
- Hidden exploration.

### Dream Nail Progression

- Dream Nail (base): from Seer.
- Awoken Dream Nail: essence trade with Seer (2400 essence).
- Unlocks dream bosses + true ending.

### Essence

From Dream Nail + Whispering Roots + Dream bosses.
- Total ~2400+ available.
- Spent with Seer for upgrades.
- Required: 2400 for Awoken Dream Nail.

### Geo (Currency)

- From enemies, chests, Tolls.
- Spent on: charms, services, maps, nail upgrades (alongside Pale Ore).
- No cap; no decay.
- Stag ride (post-unlock): free.

## Endings

Four distinct endings:

### Ending 1: Sealed Siblings (basic)

- Beat Hollow Knight boss.
- Knight takes Hollow Knight's place, locked in Black Egg.
- Infection continues.

### Ending 2: Dream No More

- Requires: Awoken Dream Nail + sufficient essence.
- Beat The Radiance in dream.
- Knight defeats infection source; radiant ending.

### Ending 3: Embrace the Void

- Requires: Void Heart charm (completed in Abyss).
- Beat The Radiance.
- Void power awakens; dark-version ending.

### Ending 4: Delicate Flower (Godmaster)

- Godhome pantheon completion.
- Multiple variants based on flags.

Dozens including:

## Playtime Milestones

- Main quest (one ending): 25-40 hours.
- 100% completion: 50-80 hours.
- Godhome pantheons + Pantheon of Hallownest: 100-200 hours for proficiency.
- All bindings on all pantheons: expert; 300+ hours.

## Save Slots

- 4 save slots in base game.
- Per-slot difficulty progression.
- Steel Soul mode (unlocked after first clear): permadeath option.
- Classic mode: standard.

## Design Philosophy

### Gear Over Grind

No XP grinding. Fights are learnable; you don't out-level them.

### Reward Exploration Unto Itself

Even lore-only finds (tablets, Dream Nail readings) feel satisfying.

### Permanent Progress

Geo decay on shade death only. Every other unlock stays.

### Bindings as Self-Imposed Challenge

Godhome bindings let skilled players test themselves without arbitrary game-designer-imposed hard mode.

### Charms as Build System

Charms let players express style: tank, glass cannon, caster, spell-spam, nail-focus, lifeblood-focus. Replay variety without class system complexity.

## Rejected Patterns

- **Skill trees / levels**: none; abilities are discrete unlocks.
- **XP farming**: no XP.
- **Paid DLC gating**: all DLC free.
- **Difficulty settings**: bindings in Godhome serve as elegant challenge modes instead.
- **Endgame gear inflation**: max stats reached mid-late game; expertise does the rest.
