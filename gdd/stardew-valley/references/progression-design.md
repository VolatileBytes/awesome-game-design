# Progression Design — Stardew Valley

Progression is multi-axis and parallel: skills, inventory/tools, building upgrades, relationships, quests, collections, mastery, and exploration regions. No single track gates the rest.

## Skill System

Five skills, 0-10 each:

### Farming

XP: harvesting crops (most) + animal products.
Bonuses per level:
- Crop quality chance.
- Hoe/watering can efficiency.
- Fertilizer recipes.

Level 5 profession choice:
- **Rancher**: animal products +20% value.
- **Tiller**: crops +10% value.

Level 10:
- **Rancher → Coopmaster**: better coop animals + faster friendship.
- **Rancher → Shepherd**: better barn animals + faster wool/milk.
- **Tiller → Artisan**: artisan goods +40% value (meta choice).
- **Tiller → Agriculturist**: crops grow 10% faster.

### Mining

XP: break rocks, kill mine monsters.
Bonuses per level:
- Stronger picks + axes.
- Ore quality variants.

Level 5:
- **Miner**: +1 ore per vein.
- **Geologist**: gems more common.

Level 10:
- **Miner → Blacksmith**: ore bars +50% value.
- **Miner → Prospector**: coal more common.
- **Geologist → Excavator**: geodes 2x.
- **Geologist → Gemologist**: gems +30% value.

### Combat

XP: kill monsters.
Bonuses per level:
- HP +5.
- Damage modifier.

Level 5:
- **Fighter**: +10 HP, damage +10%.
- **Scout**: +50% crit chance.

Level 10:
- **Fighter → Brute**: damage +15%.
- **Fighter → Defender**: +25 HP.
- **Scout → Acrobat**: cooldowns -50%.
- **Scout → Desperado**: crit +3x damage.

### Fishing

XP: catching fish + crab pots.
Bonuses per level:
- Bar size on minigame.
- Tackle options.

Level 5:
- **Fisher**: fish +25% value.
- **Trapper**: crab pot recipes cost less.

Level 10:
- **Fisher → Angler**: fish +50%.
- **Fisher → Pirate**: treasure 2x chance.
- **Trapper → Mariner**: crab pots no junk.
- **Trapper → Luremaster**: crab pots no bait.

### Foraging

XP: gathering wild plants + chopping trees.
Bonuses per level:
- Axe efficiency.
- Forage quality.

Level 5:
- **Forester**: wood +25%.
- **Gatherer**: 20% chance double forage.

Level 10:
- **Forester → Lumberjack**: hardwood from any tree.
- **Forester → Tapper**: tappers +25% yield.
- **Gatherer → Botanist**: all forage iridium quality.
- **Gatherer → Tracker**: forage shown on map.

## Mastery (1.6+)

Post-10-skill capstone:
- Earn Mastery XP after maxing all skills.
- Unlocks mastery hall + unique recipes / upgrades.
- Optional late-game progression for completionists.

## Tools

Upgrade path per tool:
- **Base** → **Copper** → **Steel** → **Gold** → **Iridium**.
- Each upgrade: blacksmith Clint + ore bars + gold + 2-day wait.
- Iridium tier unlocks charged / AOE uses.

Tool list:
- **Hoe**: till soil.
- **Watering can**: hydrate crops.
- **Pickaxe**: break rocks.
- **Axe**: chop wood.
- **Scythe**: harvest grass + hay (no upgrade, but Golden Scythe drops in cavern).
- **Trash can** (pocket upgrade): refund from trashed items.
- **Fishing rod**: Training → Bamboo → Fiberglass → Iridium.

## Building Upgrades

### House (Robin)

- **Tier 1** (10k + 450 wood): kitchen + fridge.
- **Tier 2** (50k + 150 hardwood): nursery + bigger bedroom.
- **Tier 3** (100k + various): cellar with casks.

### Farm Buildings

- **Coop** → **Big Coop** → **Deluxe Coop**.
- **Barn** → **Big Barn** → **Deluxe Barn**.
- **Shed** → **Big Shed**.
- **Stable**: horse mount.
- **Mill**: wheat/beet → flour/sugar.
- **Slime Hutch**: slime combat farming.
- **Fish Pond**: per-fish aquaculture.

### Greenhouse

Community Center reward (or purchase-fix via Joja). Year-round crop space.

## Community Center Progression

Six rooms, 30+ bundles:

### Pantry
Seasonal crop bundles → rewards a Greenhouse restoration.

### Crafts Room
Forage + geode bundles → rewards bus repair, minecart repair.

### Fish Tank
Fish bundles → rewards glittering boulder removal (river yield).

### Boiler Room
Smelted bars + artifacts → rewards minecart repair.

### Bulletin Board
NPC-favorite item bundles → rewards friendship boost event.

### Vault
Gold bundles (2.5k, 5k, 10k, 25k) → rewards bus repair finalization.

Each bundle reward unlocks a permanent feature. Complete all = major ending cutscene + statue.

## Museum Progression

- Donate artifacts (mining + digging) + gems to Gunther.
- 95 total items.
- Milestones: 40 donations unlocks rewards; full collection unlocks Rarecrow #1 + more.

## Mines Progression

120 floors:
- Levels 1-40: grass + bugs; copper + cave flies.
- Levels 40-80: iron + dust sprites; ghosts + skeletons.
- Levels 80-120: gold + lava; shadow shamans.
- Floor 120 boss: Stone Golem rush.

Post-120: Skull Cavern (Desert, infinite floors); harder + iridium rewards.

## Ginger Island Progression

1.5+ content unlock after Community Center:
- 100+ golden walnuts to collect.
- Unlock Island field, Volcano dungeon, Island Farm.
- Post-game content: Qi challenges, Perfection rating.

## Perfection Rating

1.4+ completion metric:
- All skills max + all professions.
- Community Center + Joja not taken.
- All recipes cooked.
- All recipes crafted.
- All fish caught.
- Museum 100%.
- Monster slayer goals.
- NPCs at max hearts.
- Great-grandfather's evaluation.

100% Perfection unlocks:
- Gold rosette statue.
- Qi's special vendor.
- Honey statue.

## Character Level

Aggregate of all skills — not a distinct system. Skill levels combined = account level.

## Calendar / Schedule

- **Birthdays**: gift NPC on their birthday for 8x friendship.
- **Festivals**: specific dates; participate for social + items.
- **Quests**: Help Wanted, scripted, randomized.

## Long-Horizon Goals

Content doesn't expire:
- Year 1: survive + reach Fall for big crops.
- Year 2: Community Center completion.
- Year 3: Ginger Island + marriage.
- Year 4+: Perfection + mastery.

100+ hours typical first completion.

## Quests

- ~20 scripted main quests (story beats).
- Random Help Wanted quests (daily board).
- Heart event cutscenes per NPC (~10 per candidate).
- Special requests from NPCs (mail-delivered).

## Unlocks by Year

| Year | Unlocks |
|---|---|
| Year 1 | Bus repair (CC), Greenhouse (CC), Desert |
| Year 1 Winter | Night market (winter 15-17) |
| Year 2 | Kent returns, new dialogues, new festivals variants |
| Year 2+ | Joja ending accessible |
| Year 3+ | Community Center ending (if saved) |

Most content accessible at any year; some NPC arcs advance by year.

## Design Philosophy

### Parallel Tracks

Skills, hearts, gold, collections, exploration — all progress in parallel. No track bottlenecks another.

### No Waste

Every action gives some XP. Even accidental hoe-tills give farming XP.

### Long-Term Satisfaction

Main content takes 40-80 hours; completionist 200+. Players return over months/years, picking up where they left.

### Personal Pacing

No daily logins, no timer pressure, no seasonal wipes. Your valley is yours forever.

### Endings Honor Effort

Community Center ending is narratively meaningful. Joja ending is narratively sobering. Both are complete and satisfying conclusions — but the world lives on post-ending.

## Rejected Patterns

- **Save-file wipe**: saves persist indefinitely.
- **Energy-regen timers for gating**: can be bypassed with food.
- **Pay-to-skip**: no IAP.
- **Time-limited content**: festivals recur yearly; no missable rewards.
- **Skill respecs with pain**: Statue of Uncertainty allows free profession reset.
