# Economy & Monetization — Toon Blast

## Currencies

| Currency | Earn | Spend | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coins** | Level clears, team rewards, events, IAP | +5 moves, boosters, lives | Primary soft currency |
| **Lives** | Regen 1/30min, team gifts, shop | Play a level | Time-gated |
| **Boosters** | Events, team rewards, IAP | Pre-level or in-level | Stockable |

Toon Blast uses **Coins** where Candy Crush uses **Gold Bars** — effectively same role (premium-ish currency), but earned more liberally through gameplay.

## IAP Structure

### Coin Bundles

| Bundle | Price | Coins |
|---|---|---|
| Mini | $0.99 | 130 |
| Small | $4.99 | 900 |
| Medium | $9.99 | 2,100 |
| Large | $19.99 | 4,800 |
| Huge | $49.99 | 13,500 |
| Mega | $99.99 | 30,000 |

### Booster Packs

- **Starter Pack**: 3 rockets + 3 bombs + 3 disco balls — $4.99
- **Booster Mega Pack**: 5 of each power-up — $9.99
- **Infinite Lives**: 1hr $1.99 / 24hr $4.99 / 7day $12.99

### Piggy Bank

- Fills with 5% of every coin earned
- **Break at full** for $4.99 (pay to unlock)
- **High-conversion** psychological hook

### Team Perks (IAP)

- **Team Boost Pack**: $9.99 — gives boost to all team members for 24h
- Captains/officers most likely to buy (pro-social angle)

## Spending Sinks

### +5 Moves (Primary)

- Out-of-moves offer: **900 coins** for 5 more moves
- **~45–55% of revenue**

### Pre-Level Boosters

- **150 coins** per booster per level
- Stacks: +5 moves, Rocket, Bomb, Disco Ball

### Extra Lives

- **45 coins** per life
- **180 coins** for 5 lives
- **Team gifts are free** → lives spending lower than Candy Crush

### Piggy Bank

- **$4.99** to unlock at full
- **~25% of revenue**

### Event Passes (new)

- **$4.99 per event** for enhanced event rewards
- Drives engagement during active events

## Engagement Curve

- **Day 1–3**: tutorial, free exploration
- **Day 3–7**: first difficulty spike (~level 30–50); monetization intro
- **Day 7+**: team league engagement
- **Month 1+**: habitual daily play; piggy bank triggers fire regularly

### First-Week Funnel

1. Install → play 20–30 levels
2. Level 30: team join prompt (critical)
3. Level 40–60: first hard levels; fail + offer
4. Decide: pay, wait, or team life
5. Retention: team engagement = long-term retention

## Monetization Levers

### Pay to Skip

- +5 moves (the big one)
- Lives bypass (less common with team gifts)

### Pay for Content (limited)

- Event passes
- Starter packs

### Pay for Social Boost

- Team boost pack (pro-social)
- Boosters used during team events

### No Pay-to-Win

- All levels free-beatable (with time + skill)
- Boosters give edge, not guarantee
- Team league relies on consistent play, not spending

## Team-Driven Monetization

**Innovation vs. Candy Crush**: Peak designed Toon Blast to balance **team gifts** against **spending pressure**. Team gifts reduce friction (free lives) but team engagement drives consistent play → consistent monetization moments.

## Design Principles

- **Team life gifts reduce forced monetization**
- **Piggy bank + events** replace some "+5 moves" revenue
- **Star pressure** for league = gentle engagement driver
- **Character investment** = emotional retention > purely mechanical retention

## Retention Mechanics

### Daily Coin Task

- Clear 1 specific level
- Earn 50 coins + booster

### Daily Team Task

- Team collectively clears N
- Team-wide reward

### Character Cinematic Unlocks

- Every ~100 levels: unlock a character story cutscene
- Emotional hook

## Ethical Considerations

- Peak Games carefully avoids **hard paywalls**
- **Piggy Bank is optional** (reserve pays less well than Candy Crush's outright gating)
- **Team league** incentivizes play, not payment
- **No gacha, no lootboxes**: straightforward pay-to-skip model

## Analytics / KPI

- **DAU**: 3–5M (late 2010s peak)
- **ARPDAU**: $0.20–$0.30
- **Install → Day 7 retention**: 30%+
- **Average session**: 4–6 minutes
- **Sessions per day**: 4–7 (team-league motivated)
- **Conversion rate**: 3–5% of players spend

## Content Production

- **~20–30 new levels per week**
- **Rotating events every 7–14 days**
- **Major content update every 4–6 weeks** (new characters, new mechanics)
- **Team league**: weekly cycle (every Monday reset)
