# Progression & Meta Design — Toon Blast

## Two Parallel Progressions

Toon Blast layers two progression systems:
1. **Saga-map levels**: individual progress (like Candy Crush)
2. **Team league**: weekly team-based competitive meta

This dual structure is Peak Games' template — retention from personal + social pressure.

## Saga Map

### Structure

- **Vertical scrolling path** through neighborhoods (not episodes)
- Each **neighborhood** ~50 levels, themed cartoonishly (downtown, park, beach, etc.)
- **Character cameos** between neighborhoods
- **Milestones every 50 levels**: unlock new character + team perks

### Unlock Flow

```
Level N cleared → Level N+1 unlocks
Level 50 / 100 / 150 → new neighborhood reveal
Level 100 → Team League invitation (if not already joined)
```

### Stars

- **1 star**: clear
- **2 stars**: medium score
- **3 stars**: high score (often requires Sugar-Crush-style finale)
- Stars **contribute to Team League score**

### Gates

Unlike Candy Crush, Toon Blast has **no ticket gates**. Progression is unlimited; retention comes from life regen + team league.

## Team League (Core Meta)

### Join a Team

- After level ~30, prompted to join team
- 50 members per team
- Can create team, search team, or auto-match

### Weekly League

- **Monday–Sunday** cycle
- Team score = sum of stars earned by members that week
- **Leagues**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Master → Grand Master
- **Top 3 teams** in league: promote
- **Bottom 3 teams**: relegate

### Team Rewards

- **End-of-week**: coins + boosters based on league tier
- **Top 10 finishers**: individual bonus
- **Weekly badge**: cosmetic on team profile

### Social Pressure

- Teammates see your contribution in real-time
- "You're 23rd out of 50 this week — keep playing!"
- **Inactive members** get demoted/kicked by captain
- Drives **daily engagement** beyond solo play

## Failure & Retry Loop

### Fail State

1. Run out of moves without meeting objective
2. **Out-of-moves modal**: "+5 moves for 900 coins?"
3. Yes → continue with 5 more
4. No → life consumed, retry or wait

### Life Regen

- 5 lives max
- 1 regen / 30 min
- **Team members** can send free lives (instant, no cost)

### Team Life Advantage

Key differentiator: **requesting from teammates is trivially fast**. Team life requests often fulfilled within minutes → **reduces monetization pressure on lives but increases team bond**.

## Daily Engagement

### Daily Coin Task

- Single-level daily objective
- Clear: earn coins + booster

### Team Task (Daily)

- Team-wide objective (clear 500 cubes today)
- Completion: team-wide reward

### Weekend Event

- Bonus points for league
- Special event-only levels

## Replay Value

- **Star farming**: return to old levels for 3 stars (counts for league)
- **Daily tasks**: replay specific levels

## Onboarding

### First 10 Levels

1. **Level 1**: tap any 2 cubes → introduce blast
2. **Level 2**: tap larger group → bigger blast
3. **Level 3**: Rocket intro (blast 5)
4. **Level 4**: Bomb intro (blast 7)
5. **Level 5**: Disco Ball intro (blast 9)
6. **Level 6**: Rocket fire
7. **Level 7**: Bomb fire
8. **Level 8**: Power-up combo intro
9. **Level 9**: First obstacle (crate)
10. **Level 10**: Mixed objective

### First 50 Levels

- Introduce each obstacle one at a time
- Tutorial tooltips gradually fade
- Around level 30: team join prompt
- Around level 50: first "difficulty spike" (low 3-star rate)

### Late Game (500+)

- Complex obstacle combinations
- Team-required events
- Some levels designed for booster use

## Scaling Challenge

- Move budget tightens
- Obstacle density increases
- Color palette sometimes reduced (4 → 3 colors)
- Irregular grid shapes

## Social Layer

### Team Chat

- Real-time team chat
- Pins, mentions, emoji
- Captain / officer / member roles
- **Most active teams**: chat is constant, social bond strong

### Friend Visibility

- Facebook friends show on map
- Compare levels cleared

## Retention Curve

- **Day 1–3**: solo play, tutorial
- **Day 3–7**: team invitation converts most players
- **Day 7+**: team league weekly cycle locks in retention
- **Month 1+**: team identity strongest retention driver

## Event Layer

- **Character Story Event**: rotating — learn Bruno/Cooper/Wally backstory (casual narrative)
- **Seasonal Events**: holiday re-skins
- **Star Rush**: 48-hour 2x star event for team league boost
