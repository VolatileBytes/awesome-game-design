# Economy

## Currencies

| Currency | Earn | Spend |
|---|---|---|
| **LMD** | Stages, base, trading | Level Operators, upgrade skills, base |
| **Sanity** | Time + level-ups | Stages |
| **Orundum** | Annihilation, events, missions | Gacha (Headhunting) |
| **Originite Prime** | Paid only | Gacha, refill Sanity, skip plans |
| **Recruit Permit** | Daily missions, events | Recruitment (alt gacha) |
| **Expedited Plan** | Missions | Skip Recruitment wait |
| **Credit Points** | Login, Clue exchange | Credit Store (materials, permits) |
| **Event Currency** | Event stages | Event Shop |

## Gacha (Headhunting)

- **1 pull**: 600 Orundum (or 1 Originite Prime = 180 OP ≈ 600 Orundum equivalent)
- **10-pull**: 6000 Orundum — guarantees at least one 5★+
- **Rate-up banner**: featured 6★ rate bumped from 0.6% to 0.7%
- **Pity System** (hard): from pull 51, rate increases by 2% per pull to max ~50%
- **Pity at 99 pulls**: guaranteed 6★
- **Rate-up Limited**: some limited banners (~once/year) have exclusive pity-guaranteed Operators

### Monthly F2P Pull Budget
- Orundum from: missions, weekly Annihilation, events = ~10,000–20,000 Orundum/month
- F2P player: ~20–40 pulls per month

## Originite Prime (Premium)

- Earn: ~8 OP/day from missions (free)
- Buy: $1 ≈ 6 OP; $100 ≈ 800 OP
- 180 OP = 1 10-pull
- Monthly 30-day pass: ~$5 for 30 OP/day over 30 days
- Annual pass: ~$10/month equivalent

## Monthly Card

- 30-day subscription
- Daily login: 30 Orundum + 50 pure OP (over 30 days)
- Effectively a slow grind booster
- Popular with medium-spending players

## Annual Pass

- ~$20/month equivalent
- Bigger daily rewards + exclusive cosmetic operators/skins

## Shop Types

### Credit Store
- Spend Credit Points (from Clue Exchange) for:
  - Yellow Certificates → mats
  - Permits
  - Low-tier Originite Prime

### Certificate Shop
- Yellow Certs (from dupes) → choose specific Operators or premium mats
- Pink Certs (limited events) → exclusive skins / items

### Event Shop
- Event currency → specific rewards (Operators, mats, skins)
- Limited-time only; plan your event grinding

## Cosmetics (Skins)

- Alt outfits for Operators — Elite 2 alt visuals, seasonal skins (Summer, Festival, etc.)
- Purchased with Originite Prime + Certificate
- **Cosmetic only**: no stat effect, no gameplay difference

## Sanity Economy

- 1 Sanity = ~6 minutes real-time
- Full bar (135) = 13.5 hours
- Max loss per day without playing: 10 Sanity (cap)
- **Sanity Potion** (16 Originite Prime): refills 80 Sanity — for event grinding

## F2P Progression Pace

- Full clear of main story: ~3–6 months of daily play
- E2 promotion of a 6★ Operator: ~2–4 weeks of mats per Operator (parallel farming)
- Build a "core team" of 6 well-built 6★: ~4–6 months
- Build a deep roster (30+): 1+ years

## Monetization Design Goals

- **Never pay-to-win**: no "exclusive paid 6★ that breaks gameplay" — all featured 6★ rotate back
- **Cosmetic-heavy whaling**: skins are the major whale target
- **Time-gate, not wall**: F2P can clear all content; it just takes longer
- **Gacha is fair**: pity guarantees ensure no catastrophic bad luck

## Whale Spending Targets

- Monthly passes: $5 + $10 = $15/month baseline
- Every banner pull: $50–100 per banner
- Skin collector: $30–50 per banner season
- Yearly: $500–1000+ for collectors

## Anti-Patterns

- **6★ gacha rates are already low (0.6%)** → raising them anywhere is risky; must be done carefully
- **Limited Operators FOMO** → if missed, wait a year; creates pressure but doesn't wall content
- **Opaque pity systems** → Arknights publishes them clearly; community trusts the system
- **Pay to skip Sanity entirely** → the game sells Sanity-refill, but it's OP-gated (Originite Prime-only)

## Operator-as-Content Model

Each major event:
- 1–2 new 6★ Operators on banner
- 1 new story chapter / side story
- 1 new challenge stage / roguelite season
- Cosmetics + event shop

This **content cadence** drives sustained revenue: every 3 weeks, something new. Players spending Orundum on gacha also get story + gameplay content.

## Economic Longevity

Arknights has been running since 2019 (5+ years). The economy is designed for **very long tail**:
- Operators released 4 years ago are still viable
- Mats farming rotates — old mats remain useful
- Veterans cycle their roster; new players gradually catch up
