# Progression Design

## Ranked Ladder

Seasonal; resets monthly.

- **Ranks**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Legend
- Each rank has 10 tiers (Bronze 10 → Bronze 1 → Silver 10, etc.)
- **Stars**: win → gain stars; lose → lose stars (until a floor)
- **Rank floors**: once you hit a rank (e.g., Diamond 5), you don't drop below
- **Legend**: no tiers, just a ranking number; top players compete for global #1
- End of month: rewards based on highest rank — Card Packs, Gold, Arcane Dust

## Casual

- Unranked; matches same-MMR players
- Good for deck experimentation
- Still rewards XP + quest progress

## Rewards Track

Seasonal Battle Pass-style track:
- XP earned from playing, completing quests
- Tiers unlock rewards: packs, gold, cosmetics
- **Free track**: most content accessible
- **Paid Tavern Pass**: bonus XP, exclusive cosmetic (hero skin, board)

## Quests

- **Daily**: 2–3 per day (refresh daily, stack up to 3 max)
  - Examples: "Play 40 Murloc / Demon / etc."; "Win 3 games"
- **Weekly**: 3 per week, bigger goals, bigger rewards
- **Legendary quests**: rare, story-linked
- All quests grant XP (sometimes Gold)

## Achievements

- Per-expansion achievement lists
- Cosmetic rewards (card back, diamond card upgrades)
- Completion-driven engagement (especially for lapsed players)

## Open Packs → Collect Cards

- Packs grant 5 random cards (1 guaranteed Rare+)
- Packs come from: gold purchase, rewards, events, Tavern Pass
- Duplicate protection improved over years: certain-rarity dust back, golden-version upgrade on duplicates

## Crafting

**Arcane Dust** is the crafting resource.

- Disenchant cards you don't want → dust
- Craft specific cards:
  - Common: 40 dust
  - Rare: 100
  - Epic: 400
  - Legendary: 1600
- **Golden** versions cost more dust but are cosmetic

This lets a dedicated player **target** specific cards, even if they never pull them from packs.

## Expansion Cadence

- **3 expansions per year** (April, August, November)
- Each adds ~135 new cards
- Pre-order bundle: golden pack cosmetics + immediate legendary

## Core Set & Rotation

- **Standard rotation**: cards from expansions older than 2 years rotate out of Standard
- **Core Set**: curated rotation of 200+ cards kept in Standard; refreshed annually
- This is the "dust refresh" strategy — keeps Standard meta alive, Wild format for older cards

## Onboarding

- **Forge-style tutorial**: 6 battles teaching basic card types
- **Starter decks**: 1 free deck per class
- **First-time quest chain**: extra rewards in the first 30 days
- **Catch-up mechanics**: returning players get a free deck matching current meta

## Class XP & Hero Skins

- Each class has its own XP track
- Milestones unlock alternate hero portraits
- This encourages players to **play every class**, not just one

## Battleground Progression

Battlegrounds has its own separate rating system:
- **MMR per hero pool** (not per-class like Constructed)
- Top 4 finishers gain rating; 5th–8th lose
- Monthly reset; leaderboards

## Anti-Patterns

- **Pay-to-win pressure**: competitive Wild formats have dominant decks needing Legendaries — gating these behind paywalls is toxic
- **Too-high Legendary dust cost**: 1600 is the lower limit; raising it would kill f2p viability
- **Quest requirements that force decks you don't have**: make quests flexible or offer reroll
- **Seasonal paywall spikes**: a new expansion requiring $80+ to stay competitive alienates the f2p base
