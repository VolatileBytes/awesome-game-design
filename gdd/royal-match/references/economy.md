# Economy

## Currencies

| Currency | Earn | Spend |
|---|---|---|
| **Coins** | Levels (small drip), events, missions | Extra moves, boosters, life refills |
| **Gems / Diamonds** | Achievements, events | Premium boosters, coin bundles, lives |
| **Stars** | Complete levels | Palace tasks |
| **Lives** | Time-regen or purchase | Play levels |
| **Royal Pass XP** | Playing | Pass tier rewards |
| **Event Tokens** | Event levels | Event rewards |

## Life System

- Max 5
- Regen: 25 minutes each
- Free refills: watch ad (1 life), friend gifts, palace tasks

## Extra Moves Purchase

- Near-miss moment (last move, close to goal): "Get 5 more moves for 900 coins"
- Most revenue for match-3 games comes from this single button
- Design levels to **feel winnable** with 5 more moves, even when just out of reach

## Boosters

### Pre-Level Boosters
- Choose to start a level with power-ups already on the grid
- "Start with 1 Bomb" = 900 coins; "Start with 2 Rockets" = 2000
- Heavy monetization point; big boost to hard-level clear rates

### In-Level Boosters
- **Hand**: swap any 2 pieces (even non-adjacent)
- **Hammer**: destroy any 1 piece
- **Arrow**: fire a rocket at any row/column
- All cost coins; can be purchased mid-level

## Coin Economy

- F2P earns ~200–500 coins per level won
- Extra moves cost 900–1500
- Math: F2P typically fails a couple levels per day; if they don't buy, they take a break (life system)

## Gem Economy

- Premium currency; harder to earn
- Mostly for whales and dedicated buyers
- Converts to coins at a premium rate

## Monetization Segments

- **Whales**: $100+/month; every pass, every booster sale
- **Dolphins**: $20–40/month; occasional purchase
- **Minnows**: $5/month; one-off purchase per major obstacle
- **F2P**: never pay; play through lives + ads

### Typical Revenue Mix

- Extra moves (in-level, near-miss): ~40%
- Booster bundles (pre-level): ~25%
- Coin / Gem bundles: ~15%
- Royal Pass: ~10%
- Events: ~10%

## Ad Monetization

- Rewarded ads:
  - Watch ad for free life (cap: 3/day)
  - Watch ad for free coins (cap: 5/day)
  - Watch ad for free 5 extra moves (rare, usually only after failing)
- Interstitial ads: often disabled or reduced in premium puzzle games; ruin flow

## Pricing (Typical)

- $0.99 bundle: 500 coins
- $4.99: 3500 coins
- $9.99: 7500 coins + small bonus (most popular)
- $19.99: 20,000 coins + booster pack
- $49.99: ~55,000 coins + premium booster pack
- $99.99: ~125,000 coins + Royal Pass access

## Royal Pass

- ~$10 per pass (1 month)
- Free + Premium track
- Premium: extra coins, boosters, exclusive character, palace shortcut, XP boost

## Gift & Social

- Send lives to friends (free, generates goodwill + virality)
- Team chest contributions (Kingdom)
- Team chat with encouragement

## Free-to-Play Integrity

- F2P players **can** complete all content, just slowly
- **No pay-only walls**: every level is clearable without payment (just harder)
- **Fair booster design**: boosters help, but don't auto-win

## Anti-Patterns

- **Pay-to-progress**: if players must buy to proceed, churn spikes
- **Locked content behind gems only**: too steep, feels bad
- **Subscription fatigue**: Royal Pass at ~$10 is acceptable; more would be too much
- **Interstitial ad spam**: breaks flow; premium puzzle games avoid it

## Event Currencies

- Event tokens earn by playing during event
- Event shop: spend tokens on boosters, exclusive skins, coins
- Timed expiration: creates FOMO (tuned not to be punishing)

## Long-Tail Economy

Royal Match has thousands of levels. The economy is designed for **1+ year of engagement**:
- Early levels: easy, minimal monetization
- Mid levels: balanced monetization
- Late levels: higher difficulty, more monetization opportunities
- Content adds keep long-time players engaged
