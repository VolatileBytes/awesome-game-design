# Progression Design

## Level Progression

- **Level 1** → **Level 10,000+**
- Each level is unique; no randomly-generated levels
- Complete a level → unlock the next
- No level-skipping (without paid boost)

### Level Tiers

- **Tutorial (1–20)**: teach mechanics
- **Onboarding (20–100)**: retain new players, gentle curve
- **Engagement (100–500)**: frequent variety
- **Deep engagement (500–3000)**: where most revenue comes from
- **Endgame (3000+)**: committed players; new chapters drip monthly

## Stars

- **1 star per level** (Royal Match gives 1 star/level; different from Candy Crush's 1-3 stars)
- Spend stars on **palace tasks**
- Task costs: 1–3 stars
- Tasks gate story progression

## Palace Restoration

- Each "area" of the palace has 3–10 restoration tasks
- Complete an area → unlock next area + story beats
- Areas: Entrance Hall, Gardens, Dining Hall, Library, Kingdom Grounds, etc.
- ~1–2 new areas added per month via content updates

## Lives System

- **5 lives max**
- **Lose a life** when you fail a level
- **Regen: 25 minutes** per life
- **Free full-life refill** when palace task completed (random bonus)

This is the **core gating mechanism** — forces breaks, or pay/wait.

## Daily Login Rewards

- Day 1, 2, 3, ...: escalating rewards
- Day 7+: biggest rewards (boosters, coins, free live)
- **Streak breaks** on missed day → back to Day 1

## Daily Missions

- 3 daily missions: "Complete 3 levels", "Collect 100 cakes", "Use 5 power-ups"
- Small rewards (50–200 coins + booster)
- Refresh at midnight

## Team / Club

- Join a "Kingdom" (like a guild)
- Daily chest contributions; weekly rewards
- Chat (often censored for family-friendly tone)
- Global leaderboard per team

## Royal Pass (Seasonal)

- ~1 month duration
- Free and Premium tiers
- Rewards: Boosters, Coins, Palace tokens, characters
- ~$10 paid tier

## Events

- **Weekend events**: special themed levels
- **Seasonal events**: holiday themes (Halloween, Xmas, Summer)
- **Limited Palace chapters**: exclusive story content during events

## Anti-Patterns

- **Difficulty wall**: levels so hard players must pay to progress
- **Story gates**: if story requires too many stars, pacing breaks
- **Stamina too restrictive**: >30-min regen drives churn
- **Level-skip purchase**: visible "skip level" option is controversial in the genre

## Tutorial & Onboarding

- First 10 levels: handhold through swaps, power-ups
- Palace intro after level 5
- First free Royal Pass trial for new players
- No dialog to read — visual teaching

## Retention Design

### Day 1
- Hook with King Robert's introduction
- Reach the first palace room by level 5
- Give free boosters to sample the game at its best

### Day 3
- Unlock another palace area
- Give a surprise reward (premium booster)
- Introduce a harder level type

### Day 7
- Join a Kingdom (team)
- First "near-miss" level — show purchase options contextually

### Day 30+
- Story deeper hooks
- Weekly Royal Pass maximizes value for engaged
- Seasonal events keep content fresh

## Anti-Pattern: Content Shortages

If levels are added too slowly, the top players run out:
- **Content cadence**: ~100 new levels every 2 weeks
- Design pipeline is ~6 months ahead
- Content scaling was the core reason Candy Crush dominated — Royal Match copies the strategy
