# Economy

## Currencies

| Currency | Earn | Spend |
|---|---|---|
| **Credits** | Daily missions, events, season pass | Upgrade cards (→ boost CL) |
| **Boosters** | Playing matches with a card; daily missions | Upgrade cards (with Credits) |
| **Gold** | Small daily drip, season pass, purchase | Buy variants, credits, bundles |
| **Collector's Tokens** | CL caches, season pass | Buy specific cards from the token shop |
| **Spotlight Keys** | CL caches (ramp: every 60 CL) | Spotlight Caches — chance at 3 featured cards |

## Economy Design Goals

- **Card unlocks** feel like gifts (from caches, not purchased directly)
- **Targeting** a specific card is possible via Tokens, but slow
- **Spotlight Caches** create weekly meta hooks (3 new cards rotated each week)
- **Paid content is cosmetic** (variants) not power (cards)

## Booster/Credit Loop

Every match:
1. Play cards → each earns **Boosters** for that specific card
2. Once a card has enough Boosters + you have enough Credits → upgrade it
3. Upgrades grant CL → more caches → more cards

This loops **play** into **progression** — the cards you play most upgrade fastest.

## Spotlight Caches (The Breakthrough Economy)

Weekly:
- 3 new/featured cards rotate in
- You can spend 1 Spotlight Key to open a Cache containing one of the 3 cards (or a "duplicate pity" variant)
- Keys are earned mostly through CL caches

This solves the old problem (random new cards = bad luck = frustration) with **targetable RNG**: you get *one of three*, not *one of hundreds*.

## Variants

- Alt-art versions of cards
- **No gameplay impact**
- Sold directly for Gold or earned in events
- Community expresses personal taste / flex

## Bundle Shop

- Weekly bundles: credits + gold + variants at "discount"
- Tournaments / conquest tickets
- Avatars, titles

## Season Pass

- ~$10/month
- New Season Card (exclusive for ~1 month, then rotates to free pool)
- Gold, Credits, Variants
- Premium avatar + title

## F2P Balance

- F2P can reach top rank (Infinite) monthly
- F2P can get most cards over time; new cards are slower but reliable via Spotlights
- **Whale ceiling** is cosmetic (variants) + small advantage in targeting newest cards first

## Anti-Patterns

- **Energy-gate play**: Snap has no energy — players can grind as much as they want
- **Card power behind paywall**: would poison the meta
- **Opaque drop rates**: Snap publishes rates for all caches
- **Non-targetable openers**: pure random card unlocks would hurt retention

## Missions & Daily Loop

Daily missions:
- 3 missions per day (refresh midnight)
- Each rewards Credits (100–250)
- Takes 3–6 matches typically

Weekly missions:
- Larger objectives: "Win 10 games with X keyword"
- Rewards Credits + Gold + Boosters

Season missions:
- Tied to Season Pass
- Progress through the pass tiers
